Instructions

  • Press space to play or pause the loop
  • Use your mouse to select a section of the sound to loop through
  • Use the keys Q, W, E, R, T, Y, U, and I to jump to different points in the loop
  • After setting a loop, each jump point can be adjusted using your mouse
  • Load your own MP3 and try out something new
